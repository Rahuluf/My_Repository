EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

At SAVA we believe in a well rounded education system, one that will address the development of the mind, the body and the soul of the individual. As such, we have a number of activities that help us to achieve our this basic goal:

  • Vedic Studies. Exploring ancient Indian scriptures, philosophies, and rituals for spiritual and intellectual growth.
  • Karate Classes. Learning self-defense techniques, discipline, and physical fitness through the practice of traditional martial arts
  • Yoga Classes. Engaging in a holistic approach to well-being, incorporating physical postures, breath control, and meditation.
  • Debating Competitions. Nurturing critical thinking, persuasive skills, and public speaking abilities through organized debates and competitions.
  • Cricket and other outdoor sports. Participating in recreational activities that promote teamwork, physical fitness and strategic thinking.
